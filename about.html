COMPANY OVERVIEW

UFD is the fast growing private construction company and was established in 1992. The company started its career as a small contractor and after the struggle of few years the company got incorporated as a PRIVATE LIMITED Construction Firm in 2012. The location of company headquarters are Bahawalpur and Lahore. Since being founded, we have concentrated our efforts on constructing quality buildings and providing quality services on a varied rang of projects. These have included private and public housing, factories, offices and retail units, schools, hospitals and public building.

The well management of company is only due to dedication, hard work and team work of UFD professionals. They are well experienced and having full skills in civil engineering and related fields. The rapid growth of the firm is because UFD is committed satisfying its clients’ needs through provision of cost effective engineering solutions on time, within the budget, appropriate standards, set the standard by combining professionalism, service and quality.
